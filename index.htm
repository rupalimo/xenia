Xénia Lucie Laffely is a Swiss visual/textile artist born in the 80’ and based in Montreal.

Figurative and fleshy, her pieces evoke intimacy, vanity, consumerism and lesbian love. Through digital painting, patchwork, layering, embroidering and quilting, her pieces highlight the richness of a material lead approach of textile.

Her work has been presented in solo and group exhibitions in Canada, USA and Europe, including the NADA Art Fair in New York with Bradley & Ertaskiran, the Kunstraum Niederoesterreich in Vienna, Last Tango in Zurich, Museo dei Cedri in Bellinzona, Centre National d’Art Graphique Le Signe in Chaumont.
